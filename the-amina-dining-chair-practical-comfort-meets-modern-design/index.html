How to Care for Your amina dining chair

Like any piece of furniture, the amina dining chair will last longer if you take care of it. Here are a few maintenance tips to keep your chair looking great:

  • Regular Cleaning: Dust the wooden legs regularly to prevent buildup. You can wipe down the fabric with a damp cloth to remove spills and stains. For more stubborn stains, consider using a fabric cleaner that's safe for upholstery.
  • Upholstery Care: Vacuum the seat cushion and backrest occasionally to remove dust and crumbs. Using a soft brush attachment on your vacuum will help protect the fabric from damage.
  • Protecting the Legs: Wooden legs can sometimes scratch or lose their finish over time. Consider placing felt pads under the chair legs to prevent this, especially if you're moving the chair across hardwood or tile floors.

By following these simple steps, you can maintain the beauty and longevity of the amina dining chair.
